About Us

Rocket Pitch is a one of a kind experience to help founders grow their startup.

We are a team of founders that have been through the struggles of launching a company. We built this service with every other founder in mind. We are here to make your journey easier, and provide you with the resources to succeed.

So you launched a company and bootstrapped for as long as you can. Now what? Chances are your next route is to raise some money. Whether that is through venture capital, or angel investors, fundraising is one of the hardest aspects of running a startup. Most venture capital firms today require some sort of warm introduction before you can pitch to them, or be considered for an investment. We know how hard it is to find someone to make that intro, so we're here to make that intro for you. Let us streamline your fundraising efforts, and help you land the funding you need.

Services

Our services have been specifically designed with the founders in mind. We hope these will help to catalyze your growth, and put you on the track to further success.

Pitch Deck

Every startup needs a top notch pitch deck. Your deck needs to stand on its own. We review your current deck, help you improve it, and make it ready to be seen by top venture capital firms and angel investors.

VC/Angel Introductions

We have partnered with some of the best VC firms, and Angel investors around the world. A huge part of raising money is doing research into which firms invest in your stage, and industry. Let us do the hard work for you, and on top if it we will make the warm intros.
