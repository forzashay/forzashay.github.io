About Us

Rocket Pitch is a one of a kind experience to help founders grow their startup.

We are a team of founders that have been through the struggles of launching a company. We built this service with every founder in mind. We are here to make your journey easier, and provide you with the resources to succeed.

So you launched a company and bootstrapped for as long as you can. Now what? Chances are your next route is to raise some money. Whether that is through venture capital, or angel investors, fundraising is one of the hardest aspects of running a startup. Most venture capital firms today require some sort of warm introduction before you can pitch to them, or be considered for an investment. We know how hard it is to find someone to make that intro, so we're here to make that intro for you. Let us streamline your fundraising efforts, and help you land the funding you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

We know you have some questions. If you can't find the answer here, please email us.

  • What do you get with the basic plan?

    With the basic plan you get the following: a thorough pitch deck review from professionals. We then provide you with very extensive feedback on how to improve your deck, and make it pitch ready. We will also provide you with our next steps for you. Whether it's we think you are ready to go out and raise money, or we think you need to spend some more time gaining traction. You will also get a 1 hour phone call where we will discuss the results of your pitch deck review, and answer any and all questions you may have. This plan is the ultimate starter pack, and we reccomend it for all startups who are considering funding, but need some more guidance.

  • What do you get with the regular plan?

    This is our most popular plan. It's designed for startup's that are more seriously starting the fundraising process. We will evaluate three versions of your pitch deck, and provide you with feedback for each iteration. The process would work like this: you send us your deck, we give you feedback x3. Once your deck is perfected, we will send it out to be reviewed by a VC or angel. This will give you some good feedback on what an investor thinks about your deck. You will have up to three hours of phone call consultations. Finally, we will send out your deck to two of our VC or angel partners to be seriously considered for an investment. We will be making the introduction for you, and getting your foot in the door.

  • What do you get with the premium plan?

    This is the best package we offer, and if you're serious about growing your business, we highly recommend it. You get unlimited deck reviews and feedback from us over a 45 day period. We will also provide you with feedback from three VC or angel investors. Once your deck is solid, we will forward it to five VC or Angel partners to be considered for an investment. You will receive 10 hours of phone consultations to be redeemed over a 45 day period.

  • Do you issue refunds?

    No, we do not. Rocket Pitch is a very personalized service and we are unable to issue refunds. However, if you're dissatisfied with your experience, please reach out and our team will do everything in our power to make it right.

  • How long do deck reviews take?

    7-10 business days. 3 business days with a rush fee.

  • Do you guarantee funding?

    No, we cannot guarantee funding. However, we will do everything in our power to work with you to make your startup be as attractive as possible to investors. We want our clients to land funding, and we work hard to put all the pieces together so it works out. Sometimes it just doesn't work out.
